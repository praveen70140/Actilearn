# Actilearn

ActiLearn, developed by CodeSmith, is a smart educational platform aimed to make learning more accessible and enjoyable without making it guilt free entertainment.

Here's what you can do with our software:
- Discover multiple courses spanning variety of topics and subjects
- Attempt a course and learn new concepts step-by-step
- Read, view and watch theory or do all of them with our multi-format theory
- Apply your knowledge in real time by attempting questions including MCQs, numericals, code editing and even subjective questions 
- Maintain daily streaks and compare them with others through leaderboards
- Clear your doubts by asking our AI to generate a full-fledged personalized course just for you
- Compete with friends through real time quizzes generated by AI (coming soon!)
- Create your own courses to share with others (coming soon!)
